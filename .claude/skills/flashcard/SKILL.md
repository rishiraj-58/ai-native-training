# Flashcard Drill

Generate 10 flashcard-style Q&A pairs on the topic.
Show ONE card at a time.
Wait for the user to answer (or say "reveal").
Score their response and show the model answer.
Track score: X/10 at the end.
Flag cards they got wrong for re-drill.
